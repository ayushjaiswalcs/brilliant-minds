Meet Your AI-Age Teacher

Dr. Anju Jaiswal Ph.D. (Electronics) · M.Tech · B.Ed

Dr. Anju Jaiswal brings 20+ years of teaching experience — from primary classrooms to a decade as Assistant Professor of Electronics, and now as a Computer Science teacher (TGT) at Study Hall School, Lucknow, where she also leads the school's AI Club and Robotics Club. Her teaching philosophy is simple and warm: every child can excel when concepts are made clear — not memorised, but truly understood.

At Brilliant Minds, she personally teaches every batch, knows every student by name, and keeps parents involved with regular updates and monthly assessments — with AI tools woven into everyday teaching.
